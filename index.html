JAMES THANG

After graduating college I started working as Rental Agent at Capps Van and Truck Rental. As a side hobby, I'm also a music producer at Thang Studios. I play Piano, Guitar, Bass and Drums. If you are looking to hire a new developer (or need a partner to collabrate on side projects) please reach out to me. I'm very excited to work with other developers to create great software.
